Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 4, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

FastFlex is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We believe your health data belongs to you and only you.

Overview

FastFlex ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the FastFlex mobile application (the "App").

Information We Do NOT Collect

FastFlex is designed to respect your privacy completely. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect your fasting data, journal entries, or weight information
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or advertising services

How Your Data is Stored

All data you enter into FastFlex is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Your fasting timers and history
  • Journal entries and notes
  • Weight tracking information
  • App preferences and settings

Your data is automatically backed up to your personal iCloud account using Apple's standard backup mechanism. This backup is:

  • Encrypted by Apple
  • Only accessible to you through your Apple ID
  • Never accessible to FastFlex or any third party
  • Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

Subscription Information

If you subscribe to FastFlex Premium, your subscription is managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to verify subscription status, but:

  • We only receive anonymous subscription verification (active/inactive status)
  • We do not receive your payment information
  • We do not receive your Apple ID or personal details
  • All payment processing is handled by Apple

Your subscription information is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, FastFlex will send you local notifications on your device when you reach your fasting goals. These notifications:

  • Are generated locally on your device
  • Do not involve any external servers
  • Do not transmit any information
  • Can be disabled at any time in Settings

Children's Privacy

FastFlex is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this App.

Data Security

Because all your data is stored locally on your device and backed up to your personal iCloud account, the security of your data depends on:

  • Your device's security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Your Apple ID security
  • Apple's iCloud security measures

We recommend keeping your device secure with a strong passcode and enabling two-factor authentication for your Apple ID.

Third-Party Services

FastFlex uses the following third-party services:

Apple App Store

For app distribution and subscription management. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

RevenueCat

For subscription verification only. RevenueCat receives anonymous subscription status information but no personal data. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

iCloud

For automatic data backup to your personal iCloud account. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Notifying you through the App (if significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store your data, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: All your data is on your device and accessible to you at all times
  • Deletion: You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data
  • Portability: Your data is stored in standard formats on your device
  • Control: You have complete control over your data

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: fastflexfasting@gmail.com

Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Other applicable privacy laws

Because we do not collect personal data, many regulatory requirements do not apply to FastFlex.
